Fiddle Leaf Fig Tree

We can fig-ure this out!

Origins: They normally grow in the West African rainforest

Light: They like medium light so put in a warm spot away from direct sunlight through window

Water: Like many others, just check once a week, and if the top layer of the soil is dry, please water lightly. Check more often during the summer as they get dry quicker. They do however like absorbing moisture through their big leaves, so mist them regularly

Soil and Repotting: As they are big growers and the roots wrap around the soil, you will need to repot them every 1-2 years with additional soil to support their growth

Food: Feed with fertiliser to, once a month in Spring and Summer

Killability: They are gloriously evergreen if you keep them happy - winter is the tough one
