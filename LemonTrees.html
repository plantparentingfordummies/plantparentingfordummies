Lemon Tree

When life gives you lemons, make some sweeeeeet lemonade!

Origins: Lemon trees are thought to have come from a more bitter fruit grown originally in North-east part of India

Light: Loves light so good to outside in the summer, or inside near a sunny window or bright and warm spot in Winter

Water: The soil needs to be lightly moist so water when the top layer of soil is dry - More during summer

Soil and Repotting: If the growth appears fine, no need to report from the nursery pot, just find a decorative pot that goes with your style

Pruning: Remove dead foliage as and when and light give a hair cut each year to keep the nice bushy shape

Killability: Harder to kill, the older and more mature they get folks!
