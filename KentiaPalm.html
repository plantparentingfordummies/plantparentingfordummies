Kentia Palm

You won't be face palming yourself with this one!

Origins: Native to coast of eastern Australia

Light: Needs lots of bright light but please keep away from harsh direct sunlight

Water: This palm only needs light watering, so once a week, check whether the two inches of soil feel dry and if so, lightly water

Soil and Repotting: Like other pals, they grow slowly and will take over 10 years to reach full height and potential, so keep and eye on the bottom of the pot for exposed roots every year

Food: Need a monthly dose of fertiliser feed in spring and summer

Killability Factor: 1/10
