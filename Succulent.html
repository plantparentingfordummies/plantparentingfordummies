Succulent

Your life may succ without this!

Origins: Succulents are found in every continent where there are warm climates and propensitive to drought, apart from Antartica

Light: They need bright, well-lit spots, away from direct sunlight so that their leaves don’t burn. During winter, keep them in warm rooms away from drafts as they don’t do well in the cold

Water: Their fleshy leaves retain water, so they only need light watering. Give water only when the soil is completely dry and drain any excess water

Soil and Repotting: They would not need repotting as most varieties don’t grown further

Food: They need succulent/cactus specific feed once per month in spring and summer

Killability Factor: Very Low
