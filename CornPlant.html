Corn Plant

Corn puns are A-Maize-ing!

Origins: Native to Northern Tanzania and Mt Killamanjaro. They used to be used to mark sacred sites

Light: Happy in any light conditions, other than direct sunlight so keep away from sunny windows

Water: Low maintenance with water. Top tip - put your finger into the soil weekly and if it comes out very dry, then lightly water, otherwise all good. But make sure to mist this one every two days as it does need moisture on the leaves
