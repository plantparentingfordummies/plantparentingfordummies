Ask the Plant Doctor Find a Plant
Origins: : Native to Europe, western Asia and Northern Africa. They are seldom people from the UK who have not come across this beauty in their life

Light: : Loves bright and indirect life, so great for a hanging from a balcony or near a warm spot

Water: : Needs to be watered regularly so that the soil stays wet and not soggy. If the top layer is dry, please water. It will be handy to also have a mister so that you can maintain the humid conditions the Ivy is used to

Soil and repotting: : It takes a fair while for it to fill a normal sized pot with its roots. When you need to repot (perhaps every 2 or 3 years) you can do so at any time of the year using standard soil, or basic potting compost

Food: : If you want to help it grow faster, feed liquid fertiliser once every month in spring and summer

Pruning: : They grow fairly fast and can twist and turn, so feel free to give a haircut as required. Help it grow in a specific shape by adding a climbing stick to the pot
