Calathea

Flying the rainbow flag!

Light: Happy in any light conditions other than direct sunlight

Water: Check once a week if top layer of soil is dry, if so, provide some water. Less likely to be thirsty in the winter. Keep near a bathroom for the humidity they are used to, but if not, mist them every 2 days
